Smart Water Management System

Open University SNT Building - IoT Solution

Project Overview

This project was implemented at the Open University SNT Building to completely eliminate water wastage. Often, taps are left half-closed by users in a rush, or they break during operational hours. If left unnoticed, especially overnight, this causes massive water loss.

To solve this, I developed a system that divides the day into specific time periods to identify standard water usage patterns. If the system detects an override in these patterns (e.g., continuous flow beyond a threshold), it identifies it as a leak and automatically triggers a 12V Solenoid valve to shut off the water supply.

Smart Usage Pattern Algorithm

07:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Status: High Usage (Morning)

Leak Threshold: > 20 mins continuous flow.

Action: Close Solenoid

10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Status: Low Usage (Working hours)

Leak Threshold: > 10 mins continuous flow.

Action: Close Solenoid

12:00 PM - 02:00 PM

Status: High Usage (Lunch Time)

Leak Threshold: > 20 mins continuous flow.

Action: Close Solenoid

02:00 PM - 03:30 PM

Status: Low Usage (Working hours)

Leak Threshold: > 10 mins continuous flow.

Action: Close Solenoid

03:30 PM - 05:00 PM

Status: High Usage (Leaving time)

Leak Threshold: > 30 mins continuous flow.

Action: Close Solenoid

05:00 PM - 08:00 PM

Status: Very Low Usage

Leak Threshold: > 10 mins continuous flow.

Action: Close Solenoid

08:00 PM - 07:00 AM

Status: Unoccupied

Leak Threshold: ANY minor flow detected instantly.

Action: Immediate Solenoid Close

IoT Integration & Web Dashboard

The core of this system is the ESP32 Microcontroller, utilizing its built-in WiFi to seamlessly connect to a custom Web Dashboard. This provides real-time monitoring and analytics:

  • Data is uploaded to the dashboard every 2 minutes.
  • Displays the Real-time current water flow rate.
  • Upon detecting a leak, it locks the flow rate data. Until the Reset Button is pressed, the UI calculates and displays exactly how much water was saved versus what would have been wasted.

Hardware Components

  • ESP32 Main Controller
  • YF-S201 Water Flow Sensor
  • DS3231 Precision RTC Module
  • 12V Solenoid Valve
  • LCD Display with I2C
  • Relay Module
  • Buzzer & LED Indicators
  • Hardware Reset Button
