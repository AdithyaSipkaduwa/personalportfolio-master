Engineering Competencies

Specializing in complete EDA lifecycles, my hardware development workflow encompasses structured schematics design, rigorous circuit simulations, and precise component footprint design and assignment. I execute optimized high-density PCB routing alongside structural PCB optimization paths to safeguard high-frequency layouts. Proficient in handling industry-grade design tools such as KiCad and EasyEDA, my design portfolio features complex multiple layers architectures focused on minimal noise cross-talk and stable thermal regulation profiles.
