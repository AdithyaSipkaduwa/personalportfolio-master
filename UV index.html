Future Implementation: 24-bit ADC Upgrade

To fully leverage the Beer-Lambert Law, which requires detecting very minute variations in light intensity (absorbance), we need higher precision. The standard 12-bit ADC of the STM32F411CEU6 is the limiting factor. Upgrading to an External 24-bit ADC will allow us to capture micro-fluctuations, drastically improving accuracy.
