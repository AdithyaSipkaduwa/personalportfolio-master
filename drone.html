Project Hybrid Drone

Advanced Hybrid Heavy-Lift Drone integrating Combustion Engine Power Generation with High-Capacity Lithium Backups for Unlimited Range & Heavy Payload capabilities.

Drone Concept Art

Key Applications

🚀 High-Speed Courier & Delivery

Overcomes standard 30-minute flight time limits. The onboard engine continuously charges the battery, allowing for inter-city heavy package deliveries without needing recharge stops.

🏢 High-Rise Apartment Drops

Designed to interface with balcony "Drone Pads". Residents in tall apartments no longer need to go to the ground floor. The drone handles extreme vertical lifts effortlessly using hybrid bursts.

🎯 Military & Tactical Payload

Capable of carrying heavy payloads over long distances. Battery mode can be engaged for silent, stealth approaches after traveling via engine power.
